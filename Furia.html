Furia Esports

Furia has been in the scene for multiple years now, since the introduction of SAM to Rocket League all the way back in 2018, Season 7. Since then, the team always had sprinkles of success here and there but it was never enough for the big coop. Always shifting around their members, Brazilian 100%, it has never been enough to at least reach a grand final on the international stage. After a mixed last season, Furia were looking on the market once again for new talent to add to their roster. Joining the long-standing player in the team, yanxnz and the previous acquired Lostt, drufinho joined them in late 2023 looking to finally make the ultimate SAM team. And what a beginning it was, being super successful in SAM, reaching all the way to the top 8 losing to one of the super-French teams in Vitality in Copenhagen to make a decent showing once again in London, only losing to the later champions G2 in the semis.

It looks like every time they play infront of the crows they seem to give 120%, but are these 120% enough to finally win SAM a trophy on international stage, yet even the World Championship?
