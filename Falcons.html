Team Falcons

Team Falcons, long and running since 2021 in the scene now. With their OG member Trk511 still kicking around the Team was looking to reinforce their Team with 2 new Members after a disappointing Worlds performance. The Team consisting of Trk511, Ahmad and OKhalid back then only managed to place in 5th-8th and in general a rather disappointing season the Team was looking to build around Trk511. And if you need 2 MENA players who could bring you to new heights there was only one option, since they come in a pack. The twins Rw9 and Kiileerz joined the ORG before the 2024 season started. And with what a bang they came in. Not losing a single series in their region through 6 regionals the hopes were high in the two majors. Disappointingly the Team couldn’t perform to their expectations in Copenhagen, or the Blue Wall was just to hard to overcome. Nevertheless, they managed a top 2 finish in London but fell short to G2.

Now with Worlds just mere days away, and only 1 MENA spot in the running they seek out to make their region proud and win the whole thing, but are the nerves now better than they were before or will they crumble on the stage once again?
