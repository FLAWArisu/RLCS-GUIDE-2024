Team BDS

Here we are, the first world champions of the circuit area. Always being mistaken as an French ORG, the swiss are looking for a comeback. With the new super talent, dralii on board replacing Rise after a 2nd place finish last season, this season has been filled with up and downs. There has been no real consistency, sometimes winning regionals just to get knocked out hard at top 8 the next one. The majors have been even worse. Not a single top 4 has been achieved infront of the crowd but still they held together, and rightly so. In late August during EWC, they showed their potential, finally. Winning the entire thing beating teams like Falcons along the way to secure the big pot. But can they do it at worlds too?

Can they finally break through the top 8 and show everyone what they are capable of, potentially winning worlds, or will they crumble once again under the pressure and lead into a crazy transfer off-season?
