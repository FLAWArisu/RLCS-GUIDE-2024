What Happened Last Year?

It has been over a year since we crowned our last world champion back in Düsseldorf. After having a disastrous year before the spring split, Team Vitality managed to snatch the prodigy Alexis "zen" Bernier away from other big French clubs.

The impact? Immediately noticeable. Along with zen, Vitality managed to win the spring split and worlds unbeaten, resulting in a 5-tournament-long win streak. But back to worlds.

In the biggest event in terms of live and online audience, the hype was immense. No disappointing games, just a bunch of passionate fans and teams creating an unbelievable atmosphere.
