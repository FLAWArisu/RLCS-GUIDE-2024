Team Vitality

Let’s go one with the current World champions. Coming in only as EUs 3rd seed this time the oh so undefeatable bees of the last season have crumbled down to placing behind BDS and Gentle Mates. But want went wrong, everyone is still on the roster… except their coach. The Hornets have been on an up and down throughout the season, winning a regional championship but quickly going down to placing 3rd-4th or even only in the top 8. They haven’t missed a Major so far, but they “only” managed to get to the top 8, and weren’t close to winning it all. Most teams would call this a huge success, but for them, as cheesy as it sounds, the V stands for Victory. Of course it’s hard to find new motivation if you already achieved the biggest title, but zen has to been one of the hungriest players out there when it comes to trophies because this man just doesn’t stop.

But can all this eager be enough to give the trio their 2nd consecutive championship or will it once again be not enough at Worlds to get to the top?
