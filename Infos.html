RLCS Worlds Guide 2024

When? Where? Who? What?

A lot of questions before the incoming worlds are still swirling around. Let’s answer the easy ones first. Worlds this year will be in ‘Murica in Fort Worth, a throwback to most RLCS fans since we have already been there. Between the 10th of September and the 15th of September, the 16 best teams will duke it out once again.

Since we are in America, the times obviously aren’t too friendly for EU viewers. With a new format and fewer teams this year compared to last, it will truly be highlight game after highlight game.

In total, these 16 teams are split into 7 regions:

  • 5 EU teams
  • 4 NA teams
  • 2 SAM teams
  • 2 OCE teams
  • 1 MENA team
  • 1 APAC team
  • 1 SSA team
