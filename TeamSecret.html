Team Secret

Second seed in South America and… Another fully Brazilian team? Yes, for the first time SAM only sends Brazilian players to worlds. Team Secret, formerly been in EU as well, now a fully pledged SAM team, have established them as the second best team. After a nail biter last regional, Team Secret managed to clinch the second spot over Complexity by just 2 points. With their OG member Kv1 and 2 new members aboard the Team had a mixed season which started to become better as longer as the season got, capping of the last regional with a win over the dominant Furia. Missing out on Copenhagen completely, the boys were eager to show what they can do in London but sadly it just wasn’t enough. The EWC didn’t spark much joy either, but as any other Brazilian would be, they are still super stoked to show the world that SAM is not a 1-Team-Region.

Can they excel like their brothers did this season or will their form continue?
