OG Esports

Coming in, a new and HUGE ORG which entered Rocket League this season. Barely clinching the 4th seed for NA in front of Luminosity Gaming, OG will be hyped and stoked to even be here. The team behind this huge name are basically the “left overs” of the region and by far the oldest bunch at worlds this year, coming in at a combined age of 23. The whole season for them has been, well quiet. After a horrendous start to the season going 0/3 in the first major, OG have been the first and only team coming in last in a qualifier but still managing to qualify for a major. They managed to qualify for both Copenhagen and London, the hopes were high after being a clear top 4 team. Sadly the performances at both majors have been severely lacking, not even reaching Top 8.

Now, without any EWC experiences in the off season, and haven’t had a pro game in almost 3 months, the old guard want to show that experience matters, but can they finally pull it off or will they once again crash out before top 8.
