Pioneers

The 2nd seed OCE is sending this time is Pioneers. After a decent season the team still decided that the have to look for a new member to replace the kicked Scrub. The probably best player on that squad was replaced with Superlachie, who with no doubt, showed that he deserved to be there. Still, the Pioneers were never more than second to PWR and dueling until the end for the second spot with Chiefs. After a lot of drama, including replacing Fiberr with their coach for the last regional, the team will try their best at worlds. Only going 1/3 in Copenhagen and missing out of London completely doesn’t promise much, but the boys in yellow will look to do their best to at least come to the last Swiss round, hopefully overthrowing PWR.

Is it possible for them or will the results repeat themselves and the progress will be reset by the team just like they did by themselves last season?
