Gen.G Mobil1 Racing

To the moon to NA’s 3rd seed, or something like that. NA had a big problem of only their top 2 teams performing in an international settings this season. Spacestation have been on the up though. Struggling in the first half, not even managing to qualify for the major, missing out as 5th seed due to unfortunate circumstances. What are those circumstances? Until the 6th Qualifier, Spacestation always had bad luck running in those top 2 teams, Geng and G2. Before that Qualifier, Spacestation haven’t lost to ANY other team besides those two. LJ and Hockser have been improving all season long finally locking in a spot for a major where they sadly fell short again to G2 after showing a lot of potential. Now after another “meh” performance of them in the EWC they finally want to break their self-inflicted curse and at least break into the top 4 in a LAN setting.

But are they capable of doing that or will they be haunted again by their nightmares, maybe forcing a move in the off season to finally break the shakles?
