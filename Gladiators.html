Gaimin Gladiators

Lets head over to the Asia region, the most populated region in the world. Being in RLCS for almost 4 years now, the region still hasn’t seen big success. The most came last season from Elevate where they went 2/3 in group stage in the wildcard almost positively upsetting everyone. Now, a year later, their biggest rivals, Gaimin Gladiators snatched away that sole spot APAC provides. But it came at a big cost. Only having one person originally coming from the APAC region and alongside 2 imports they managed to secure their worlds spot in the last regional they played. Now, coming to worlds for the first time to the main event, and the first time in front of the crowd, they come in to impress.

But can they finally manage a miracle run, pushing their way into the first APAC top 8 or will they fall apart like the countless times before?
