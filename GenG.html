Gen.G Mobil1 Racing

Coming in hot is NA’s 2nd seed for worlds. GenG has become a staple in the Rocket League scene joining now almost 2 years ago. Still keeping onboard two of their “founding members” the duo of AppJack and Chronic were looking to change things up after the 2023 season went downhill since their win at the fall major, only placing 5th-8th after being dismantled by Karmine Corp. And who better than one of the best NA players out there, Firstkiller. With him Geng have been looking better this season but they are clearly only the number 2 in NA after G2, which is also reflected in the standings. Nowhere near G2 but also miles away from 3rd place. Sadly the Major performances haven’t been as successful as they hoped them to be, not being in the challenge for the title at all so far.

Can they finally find their Rhythm they had in the Regionals or will they further proof that NA is only second fiddle to Europe?
