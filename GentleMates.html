Gentle Mates Alpine

Gentle Mates Alpine are a standout team in the RLCS 2024 season. The spring-split champions of 2024 secured the 1st seed for EU with their exceptional LAN performances. Though their journey ended in the semi-finals in London and the EWC tournament in August was a setback, Seikoo, Itachi, and Juicy are eager to showcase their skills in front of the American crowd. With Itachi's clever play, Seikoo's surprising maneuvers, and Juicy's mechanical prowess, they are once again strong contenders for the trophy.
