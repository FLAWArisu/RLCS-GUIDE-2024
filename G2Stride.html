G2 Stride

We have been talking about the Spring split champions already, so let's hop on over to the current defending summer split champions. After losing the spring split in the finals, the three boys of Atomic, Beastmode, and Daniel were looking forward—no roster changes needed. With a dominant NA campaign, only placing 1st except in 1 of the 6 regionals, they were eagerly looking forward to London. And rightly so. Looking dominant all weekend, the only real team that could challenge them for the win were Gentle Mates or Falcons.

In the end, G2 had to wither down Falcons in the finals, winning without a doubt. However, at EWC, their performance faltered with their sub Nass. Now back together, they aim to win Worlds and look dominant enough to do so.
