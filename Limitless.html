Limitless

Now over to our newest and, surprisingly most discussed reason. Why is that? Well Rocket League hasn’t managed to introduced a community wanted 2/3 rule. What’s that? We want to enforce that 2/3rds of the roster have to be from the region they play in. Since that isn’t the case, EU has managed to invade the SSA region which led to many controversies. Luckily in the end, we got a proper SSA team at LAN. Limitless have always been the top dogs of SSA, never really being challenged for that LAN spot. Sadly due to South African energy saving, the pros aren’t really able to consistently improve and play other high level player due to the region being new. Nethertheless, SSA always came to LAN to proof themselves. Limitless, being it most of the time, have shown that they always strive to improve but sadly have always fallen short, never managing to win a single series in the main event.

Can this time be a different scenario? Can Limitless finally clutch that one series win they always have been after or can they even try to achieve more?
