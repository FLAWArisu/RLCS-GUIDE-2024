Karmine Corp

WHAT’S THE COLOR? BLUEEE! Coming in as EU’s 4th seed we have the crazy players coupled with even crazier fans. No one would’ve thought that after picking up Rise and Atow that this super team would qualify for worlds as 1st Seed, but a disastrous show in the 2nd half of the season almost meant certain death if it wasn’t for Oxygen’s choke at the London major. Now, after having plenty of preparation time, the boys in blue surely want to pick up their pre Copenhagen form, winning every regional with never seen gameplay. The chemistry has always been high between these 3, even more their emotions they show on and off the field. Going on a tear in the EWC qualifier, truly taking everyone apart and showing really strong performance in the scrims, let’s the hope for the Blue Wall reign high once again.

But can the trio of French speaking players succeed in those expectations, or will the wall crumble once again when the pressure gets high? Only time will tell.
