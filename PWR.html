Furia Esports

Oh OCE, the longest region that has been added after the two OG regions of NA and EU. After SAM and MENA, other exciting regions have been added way later and sharing their success greatly so far, OCE hasn’t been the same since season 6, where Chiefs Esports managed to place an astonishing 4th place. Since then OCE teams have struggled to even get close to the top 8. But not this season, PWR managed to build a super-team roster from the crème de la crème from the OCE region and has been quite impressive so far. In the probably most hardest season to get a top 8 so far, they have shown immaculate potential. Two times being so close going out in Swiss stage 2-3 have only given them a 9th-12th finish. With an unchanged roster throughout the season, they managed to “conquer” their region, almost doubling the points of the 2nd placed team.

Now coming in after probably fighting kangaroos and snakes since the last major, they seem ready to finally break the OCE curse and show once again that they can be equals to at least SAM and MENA, if not even more.
